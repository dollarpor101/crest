ABOUT US

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting innovative projects in technology, lifestlye, education, and environmental sustainability.

Our grants help individuals and teams turn ideas into reality, fostering positive change in communities worldwide.

The Crest Foundation is a philanthropic organization that works to dramatically reduce disease and poverty and build a more equal world.

We invest our expertise and resources in organizations, research institutions, and global alliances that are fueling progress toward a future that is free of infectious diseases, provides economic opportunity for all, and ensures the health of mothers and babies.

Our charitable support, which in 2024 totaled more than US$8 billion, focuses on innovative products, ideas, and approaches that have the potential to save and improve the greatest number of lives.
